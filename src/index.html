RAPT



The Republic of Trasjasia is a nation located in the heart of Central Asia, formerly part of the already dissolved Union of Tarban Socialist Republics (UTSR). With a territory rich in mineral resources and with significant oil and water reserves; since its independence in 1990 Trasjasia has struggled to consolidate a strong state and a stable economy that brings welfare to its population. However, the Trasjasian Socialist Party (TSP) has co-opted the TrasjasIan democracy through the presidential figure, which has been in power for over three decades. Given the situation of corruption, economic inequality, ethnic frictions and territorial conflicts that are experienced in Trasjasia, the Revolutionary Army of the People of Trasjasia (RAPT) emerges as a movement of popular and independent organization that seeks to achieve dismissal of the current president, to call for new democratic elections and free the Trasjasian territories from the Tarbana influence; with the aim of improving the lives of its inhabitants.
